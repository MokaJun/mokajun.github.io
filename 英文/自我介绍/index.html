Contradiction

由于部分工作分配不合理、组员工作积极性较低，疫情原因，隔离，导致工作效率较低。

some assignment of the project is not reasonable

team members have low work motivation due to pandemic break out and lockdown

很多时候讨论不在一个频道上，双方经常出现无法理解或误解对方的情况。

Our discussion is not always on the same channel, which brings misunderstanding and miscommunication between us.

    - 解决：

        一次讲不通就多讲几次，直到把问题和方案明晰。

        If you don’t make sense at a time, talk about it several times until you clarify the problem and the plan

4.1.1 10人团队如何分工？到底是根据组员兴趣和能力？还是根据实际需求？

​ how to distribute work among team members?

​ rely on either their ability and interest. If no one take a specific task, then we have to rely on actual requirements in the project.

组员兴趣

​ 利：动力化为效率

​ motivation from the interest brings productivity.

​ 弊：不见得能把需求面面俱到

​ may not have time or energy to do everything for the requirement of project

原本分配给一个人的工作被分配给了4个人后，人均工作量相对减少。但新加入的三位组员由于个人学业繁忙等原因并未全心全意投入到工作中，所有几乎每周的大部分工作量都是在每周周会现场完成的，这几大的耽误了我和另一位老组员的时间。

3 members prefer to finishing most of their assignment during the weekly meeting, which wastes the time of other team members which finishes assignment on time.

        - 解决方法：

            根据组员的实际情况（能力、精力和时间）尽可能地减少新进三位组员的工作量并降低他们的工作难度，将工作量集中于核心组员身上。

            reduce the workloads and difficulties of that 3 members.

Thoughtworks

公司是做软件设计和交付

It provides software design and delivery, consulting services and outsourcing services.

ThoughtWorks 给我的印象非常好，再加上比起薪资我更看中宽松的工作环境和充裕的个人时间

ThoughtWorks impressed me a lot, compare with high salary, I prefer a more relaxed working environment and adequate personal time.

9.1 贵公司有很多吸引我的理念。

​ Your company has a lot of attractive ideas for me

​ 9.1.1 非常想学习贵公司的“敏捷开发（Agile Development）”模式，学会如何高效地管理项目组。

​ 9.1.2 贵公司能够加强个人对TDD（Test-Driven Development） + 单元测试（unit testing）的认识，提升代码重构(Code-Review)能力，并使代码更加整洁

​ 9.2 贵公司非常重视员工个人能力的提升

​ Prioritize the improvement of employees’ ability 　

​ 9.3 外企不会经常加班，有更多的时间提升个人能力

​ International companies do not often work overtime, which gives employees more time to improve own ability.

Career Plan

我想留在XX，因为那里发展潜力大，视界开阔。对于未来的规划目标是：进入公司，做一年的程序员，第二年升为初级软件工程师并申请海外业务岗位，第四年升为高级软件工程师，第六年升级为架构师，第八年开始成为精选项目经理，再后来就是总监、总经理。

I want to stay in Thoughtworks because international company brings me more opprotunities to communicate with software engieers from different countries. The Career plan for the future is: enter the company and be a programmer for first year, be promoted to junior software engineer in the second year and apply for overseas business positions, be promoted to senior software engineer in the fourth year, and be promoted to architect in the sixth year. Became a selected project manager in the eighth year, and then the program director.
