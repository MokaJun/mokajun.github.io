Dear interviewers/audience, here is Zhibin. Today I’m going to introduce Artificial Intelligence. This presentation is divided into 3 main parts. Firstly I would like to generally talk about What Artificial Intelligence (AI) is. Next part will be The Evolving Stages of Artificial Intelligence. After that, I’m going to show you AI on practical level. So,What Does Artificial Intelligence (AI) Mean?

从人工智能的定义 What Does Artificial Intelligence (AI) Mean?

PPT*：a branch of computer science dealing with the simulation of intelligent behavior in computers

AI contains massive subjects. From calculator to driverless car, even include some technology that can evolve society in future, AI is related to a wide set of aspects, so people often doubt that what AI is. The formal definition of Artificial Intelligence is the capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior. AI is not a single subject, it include many disciplines such as machine learning, computer vision, natural language understanding (NLU) and natural language processing (NLP).

Actually, AI has been used frequently in human daily life. I’ll give you some instances to help you understand AI more clear. For example, Google is a weak AI search engine and smartphone system is a weak AI as well. Furthermore, massive car companies** have used numerous AI technologies to avoid accidents. Even e-mail filter is a weak AI too. Therefore, AI is close to us and perform an increasingly important role in our life.

I think I have made a clear point about what AI is, Let’s move on. The next part is..

The Evolving Stages of Artificial Intelligence

The demand for faster and more efficient information processing is growing exponentially as AI becomes more prevalent in business applications. Conventional digital processing method cannot keep up with this demand. That is why researchers are taking inspiration from the brain which can process information with adaptive learning capabilities in an efficient, scalable manner. AI is created and evolved because we need this technology to create more commercial value. We can generally divide AI into 3 main evolving stages.

Weak AI is capable of performing only a limited set of predetermined functions.

For instance, AlphaGo can beat chess world champion , but he only has this one function, nothing else. If you talk with him, he may not do react with you. Next point.

Strong AI is said to equal the human mind’s ability to function autonomously according to a wide set of elements;

At this stage, Strong AI has same capability as human, which means it can do anything that human can do, even include sensing world and obtaining emotions. We have not achieve this stage. From weak AI to strong AI, there is a huge gap. For machines, logic is easy but sensing is hard. Currently, Google is trying to create an AI to understand fairytale book as 6-years-old child, and it already cost millions dollar. However, even Google complete the project, it just a start for strong AI. To achieve real human level, AI must get advanced intelligence such as emotion, feeling and morals.

Super AI is expected one day to exceed human intelligence (and conceivably take over the world). When human completely achieve strong AI, AI will evolve exponentially cause by the base value is high enough. I cannot infer what will happen. Human finally ruling this world tell us intelligence is the most powerful weapon, so I guess Super AI may take world from human? Maybe?

However, At this time, weak AI is only beginning to enter mainstream computing applications, so don’t worry about Super AI stage. Next part, I will tell you Artificial Intelligence On a Practical Level.

应用领域现状 Artificial Intelligence On a Practical Level

AI is currently being applied to a range of functions both in the lab and in commercial/consumer settings, including the following technologies:

  • Speech Recognition, it allows an intelligent system to convert human speech into text or code.

    It provide a great convenience. For instance, It can allow us lock or unlock device by our voice. We also can speak something then transfer it to email and send it. Next point is,

  • Natural Language Processing, it enables conversational interaction between humans and computers.

    As you know, this is a much more convenient manner to do interact with machine. For example, you can just say Hi Siri, then you can query her and wait her answer without typing on phone.

  • Computer Vision, this technology allows a machine to scan an image and use comparative analysis to identify objects in the image.

    We have used it to recognize face and vehicle tail number. It can save a great quantity of labor resource, which means human can move to more meaningful job.

  • Expert systems gain knowledge about a specific subject and can solve problems as accurately as a human expert on this subject.

    It rely on inferring algorithm and the plenty of data that stored in database. The database is similar as our brain, the more data it has, the more experience it has. It supports AI to deal with problem as a human expert, such as AlphaGo, AI translation, and etc.

Overall, I think AI makes a great contribution to the society. The advantages is far outweigh the drawbacks. I expect it can develop quickly, stably and reliably. That is all. Thank you for listening.
